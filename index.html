What is StatsHouse?

StatsHouse is a monitoring system that is highly available, scalable, and multitenant. One can hardly overload it—due to aggregation and sampling. Read more about StatsHouse features.

It allows your applications to send metric data without limitations, and you can view statistics on a graph in a live mode.
